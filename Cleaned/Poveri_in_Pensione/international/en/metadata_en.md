# Metadati Video - Why we will retire poorer (and how to avoid it) (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "Retirement Consumption and Pension Design" by Kolsrud, Landais, Reck, Spinnewijn, published in the American Economic Review in 2024, asks: is early retirement actually worth it? A study based on Swedish data reveals the hidden cost of pension reforms: those who stop working early experience a much more drastic drop in consumption than others. Let’s discover how current policies are reshaping the well-being of future retirees.
