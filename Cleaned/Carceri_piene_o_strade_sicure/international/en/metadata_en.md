# EN - Full Prisons or Safe Streets?

## Description
The study 'Incarceration and Incapacitation: Evidence from the 2006 Italian Collective Pardon' by Buonanno and Raphael, published in the American Economic Review in 2013, asks: what would happen if Italy suddenly released a third of its inmates? This study analyzes the sensational 2006 pardon to measure how effective prison really is at stopping criminals and whether keeping them locked up is always the best solution.
