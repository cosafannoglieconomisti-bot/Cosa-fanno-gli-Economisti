# Video Metadata - When the Church Stopped Italy (EN)

## Description
The study "Catholic Censorship and the Demise of Knowledge Production in Early Modern Italy" by Blasutto and de la Croix, published in The Economic Journal in 2023, analyzes how the Index of Prohibited Books stifled knowledge production in Italy. Through the "chilling effect," censorship pushed the best talents toward compliant but uninnovative careers, causing a collapse of Italian intellectual leadership that lasted for centuries and cost almost 43% of potential scientific production.
