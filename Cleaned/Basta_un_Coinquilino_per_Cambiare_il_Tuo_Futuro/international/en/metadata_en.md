# Metadati Video - Just One Roommate Can Change Your Future (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "Interaction, Stereotypes, and Performance: Evidence from South Africa" by Corno, La Ferrara, and Burns, published in the American Economic Review in 2022. Can a simple roommate break down racism and revolutionize your university career? In this study conducted in South Africa, we discover how assigned cohabitation between students of different ethnicities reduces prejudice and drastically improves academic performance.
