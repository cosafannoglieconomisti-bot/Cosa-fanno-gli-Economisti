# Metadati Video - Does Family Hinder the Economy? (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "Family Values and the Regulation of Labor" by Alesina, Algan, Cahuc, and Giuliano, published in the Journal of the European Economic Association in 2015, analyzes how strong family ties influence the choice of labor market institutions. It demonstrates that in cultures with strong ties, individuals prefer regulated markets to protect themselves against low geographic mobility, despite this leading to higher unemployment and lower incomes.
