# Metadati Video - Does God Block Democracy? (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "Earthquakes, Religion, and Transition to Self-Government in Italian Cities" by Belloc, Drago, Galbiati, published in The Quarterly Journal of Economics in 2016: Can an earthquake stop democracy? This research reveals how natural disasters in medieval Italy slowed the emergence of free communes by strengthening the power of bishops, who were seen as the sole mediators against divine wrath.
