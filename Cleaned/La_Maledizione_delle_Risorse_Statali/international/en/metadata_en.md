# Metadati Video - The Curse of State Resources (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "The Political Resource Curse" by Brollo, Nannicini, Perotti, Tabellini, published in the American Economic Review in 2013. What happens when an administration suddenly receives a mountain of money? This study analyzes Brazilian municipalities, revealing how federal transfers can paradoxically increase corruption and drastically lower the quality of mayoral candidates.
