# Metadati Video - Less Competition, More Meritocracy? (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "Less Competition, More Meritocracy?" by Fang, Noe, published in the Journal of Labor Economics in 2022. Are we sure that ruthless competition always rewards the best? This groundbreaking study demonstrates how overly rigid standards drive candidates to bet everything on risk, ultimately rewarding luck instead of genuine talent.
