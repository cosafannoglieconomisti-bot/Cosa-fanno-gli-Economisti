# Metadati Video - Love or Legal Status? The Truth (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "There’s More to Marriage Than Love" by Adda, Pinotti, and Tura, published in the Journal of Political Economy in 2025, analyzes the effect of obtaining legal status on marriage choices. The researchers find that access to legal status reduces the probability of immigrants marrying natives by 40% and increases the separation rate by 20%, highlighting the role of legal incentives over purely cultural preferences.
