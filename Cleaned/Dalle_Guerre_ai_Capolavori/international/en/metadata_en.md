# Video Metadata - Did Copyright Fuel the Golden Age of Italian Opera? 🎭 The Economics of Creativity

## Description
Does legal protection actually foster art? This video explores the groundbreaking study "Copyrights and Creativity: Evidence from Italian Opera in the Napoleonic Age" by Michela Giorcelli and Petra Moser, published in the Journal of Political Economy (2020). We analyze how the introduction of copyright laws in 19th-century Italy transformed the music industry, proving that intellectual property rights significantly incentivized the production of high-quality new operas.
