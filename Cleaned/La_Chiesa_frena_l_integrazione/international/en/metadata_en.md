# Does the Church Hinder Integration?

## YouTube Description
The study "Faith and Assimilation: Italian Immigrants in the United States" by Gagliarducci and Tabellini, published in The Economic Journal in 2025. Were Italian parishes in the United States a refuge or an obstacle? This study analyzes how the Catholic Church strengthened immigrant identity, improving their children's education but slowing down social and economic integration.
