# Metadati Video - Was the Mafia Born in the Mines? (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "Poor Institutions, Rich Mines: Resource Curse in the Origins of the Sicilian Mafia" by Buonanno, Durante, Prarolo, Vanin, published in The Economic Journal in 2015. Let's explore how the boom in the sulfur market, combined with weak state institutions, fostered the birth of the Sicilian Mafia. A journey through mines and fragile institutions to understand whether natural wealth was a blessing or a curse.
