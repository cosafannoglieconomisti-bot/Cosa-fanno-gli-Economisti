# Metadati Video - Do Banks Discriminate Against Women? (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "Do Women Pay More for Credit? Evidence from Italy" by Alesina, Lotti, and Mistrulli, published in the Journal of the European Economic Association in 2013: Why does a woman in Italy pay more for a loan than a man, even when she is less of a risk? Let’s explore the results of this eye-opening investigation into the gender gap within the credit market.
