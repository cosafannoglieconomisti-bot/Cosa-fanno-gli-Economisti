# Metadati Video - Meritocracy or pure luck? (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "A Meritocratic Origin of Egalitarian Behaviour" by Cappelen, Mollerstrom, Reme, Tungodden, published in The Economic Journal in 2022, explores what happens when we don't know whether someone's success depends on talent or luck. This study demonstrates that uncertainty pushes even the most staunch meritocrats toward equality, explaining the deep roots of conflicts over redistribution.
