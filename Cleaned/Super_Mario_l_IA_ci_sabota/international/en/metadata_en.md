# Metadati Video - Super Mario: Is AI Sabotaging Us? (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "Super Mario Meets AI: Experimental Effects of Automation and Skills on Team Performance and Coordination" by Dell'Acqua, Kogut and Perkowski, published in The Review of Economics and Statistics in 2023, analyzes the impact of introducing high-performance artificial intelligence on the coordination, trust, and motivation of a human team engaged in a cooperative task.
