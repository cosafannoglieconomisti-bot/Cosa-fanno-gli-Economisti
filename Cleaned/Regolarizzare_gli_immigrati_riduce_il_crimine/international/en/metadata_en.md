# Video Metadata - Legalizing Immigrants Reduces Crime: The Italian Click Days (EN)

## Description
The study "Clicking on Heaven’s Door: The Effect of Immigrant Legalization on Crime" by Paolo Pinotti, published in the American Economic Review in 2017, analyzes the causal impact of immigrant legalization on the probability of committing crimes, using the "click days" system in Italy as a natural experiment.
