# EN - Do we love taxes? A cultural question

## Description
The study 'Culture, Context, and the Taste for Redistribution' by Luttmer and Singhal, published in American Economic Journal: Economic Policy in 2011, asks: Why do some countries accept high taxes while others do not? This study demonstrates that our desire for welfare does not just depend on our wallets, but is a deep cultural value that we carry with us even when we change continents.
