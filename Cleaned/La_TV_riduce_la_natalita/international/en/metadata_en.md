## Descrizione YouTube
The study "Soap Operas and Fertility: Evidence from Brazil" by La Ferrara, Chong, Duryea, published in American Economic Journal: Applied Economics in 2012. Can television influence the number of births across an entire country? Discover how Brazilian soap operas by Rede Globo revolutionized family models, leading to a drastic decline in fertility without any government intervention.
