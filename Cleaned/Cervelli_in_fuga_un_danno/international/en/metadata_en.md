# Metadati Video - Brain Drain: A Hidden Cost? (EN)

## Descrizione YouTube
The study "The Collapse of the Soviet Union and the Productivity of American Mathematicians" by Borjas, Doran, published in The Quarterly Journal of Economics in 2012. What happens when Soviet geniuses suddenly flood the American academic market? This study reveals how a massive influx of foreign talent can, paradoxically, hinder the careers and productivity of local researchers.
